Here are some key characteristics :

Material composition :

Flat Profile : These boxes are designed to have a relatively low width and depth compared to their height. This design allows for efficient stacking and optimal use of vertical space during storage and transportation.
Tall Height :The emphasis on height makes these boxes ideal for accommodating taller or elongated items while maintaining the overall structural integrity of the packaging.

Strength and durability :

Posters and Prints: Tall shipping boxes are often used to ship posters, prints, and other flat items that need protection against bending or folding during transit.
Tubes and Cylinders: Some flat and tall boxes may have a cylindrical or tube-like shape, making them suitable for shipping items such as rolled documents, blueprints, or cylindrical products.
Vertical Products: Items that have a natural vertical orientation, such as certain tools, artwork, or display materials, can benefit from the design of flat and tall corrugated boxes.

Corrugated Cardboard: Like standard corrugated shipping boxes, these flat and tall boxes are typically made from corrugated cardboard. This material provides strength, durability, and some level of protection against external elements.
